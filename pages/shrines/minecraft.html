What is Minecraft?

In case you have been living under all of the rocks of the earth, Minecraft is a survival/sandbox game originally developed by Mojang. Its main feature is the virtually infinite world generation. Unfortunately, the game was bought by Microsoft Corporation. You can read about why I absolutely despise them here.
